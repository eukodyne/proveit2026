Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for Operating the Comexi F2 ML Flexographic Printing Press. This applies to "Press 103" equipment in the Unified Namespace (MQTT tag path "Enterprise/Dallas/Press/Press 103") 1. Purpose This SOP outlines the procedures for operating the Comexi F2 ML Flexographic Printing Press, including both single-pass and two-pass printing processes, based on the specific requirements of the Stock Keeping Unit (SKU) and the Bill of Materials (BOM). 2. Scope Applicable to all trained operators responsible for setting up, operating, and maintaining the Comexi F2 ML press in the production of flexible packaging materials. 3. Responsibilities Operators: Ensure adherence to this SOP during press operations. Supervisors: Verify compliance and provide guidance as needed. 4. Safety Precautions Always wear appropriate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE). Ensure all safety guards are in place before operating the press. Follow lockout/tagout procedures during maintenance. 5. Equipment and Materials Comexi F2 ML Flexographic Printing Press Printing Plates: Prepared as per job specifications. Inks: As specified in the BOM. Substrate: As specified in the BOM. Cleaning Supplies 6. Procedure 6.1 Job Preparation Review Job Specifications: Examine the SKU and BOM to determine the number of colors and printing sequence required. Prepare Printing Plates: Ensure plates are correctly prepared and correspond to the job specifications. Ink Preparation: Mix and prepare inks as per the BOM. 6.2 Press Setup Single-Pass Printing: Color Configuration: If the job requires up to 8 colors, set up the press for a single-pass operation. Plate Mounting: Mount the appropriate plates onto the plate cylinders. Ink Stations: Fill ink reservoirs with the specified inks. Substrate Loading: Load the substrate onto the unwinder. Registration and Calibration: Perform necessary alignments to ensure accurate print registration. Two-Pass Printing: First Pass Setup: Color Configuration: Set up the press to print the first set of colors (up to 8). Plate Mounting: Mount the corresponding plates. Ink Stations: Fill ink reservoirs with the specified inks for the first pass. Substrate Loading: Load the substrate onto the unwinder. Registration and Calibration: Perform necessary alignments. First Pass Printing: Start Printing: Initiate the printing process for the first set of colors. Monitor Quality: Continuously check for print quality and make adjustments as needed. Intermediate Handling: Unloading: After the first pass, rewind the printed substrate. Inspection: Inspect the printed substrate for any defects. Reorientation: If required, reorient the roll to ensure proper alignment for the second pass. Second Pass Setup: Color Configuration: Set up the press for the additional colors. Plate Mounting: Mount the plates for the second set of colors. Ink Stations: Prepare inks as specified for the second pass. Substrate Loading: Reload the substrate onto the unwinder. Registration and Calibration: Ensure precise alignment with the first pass print. Second Pass Printing: Start Printing: Proceed with printing the additional colors. Monitor Quality: Continuously check for print quality and registration accuracy. 6.3 Post-Printing Procedures Drying: Ensure the printed substrate passes through the drying system to cure the inks. Quality Inspection: Conduct a thorough inspection to verify print quality and adherence to specifications. Rewinding: Rewind the finished product onto cores as specified. Cleanup: Clean all ink stations, plates, and other equipment used during the process. 7. Troubleshooting Misregistration: Check plate mounting and substrate alignment. Ink Issues: Verify ink viscosity and compatibility with the substrate. Print Defects: Inspect plates for damage and ensure even ink distribution. 8. Maintenance Perform routine maintenance as per the manufacturer's guidelines. Keep a log of maintenance activities and any issues encountered. 9. Documentation Record all relevant production data, including job specifications, ink usage, substrate details, and any deviations from standard procedures. 10. References Comexi F2 ML Operation Manual Safety Data Sheets (SDS) for all inks and solvents used 11.
